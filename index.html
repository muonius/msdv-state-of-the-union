The American Promise

I came to the United States in 2012 because I was sold to the American Promise. The Promise, that all men are created Equal, that they are endowed by their Creator with certain unalienable rights that among these are Life, Liberty and the pursuit of Happiness. Today, through the lens of 10 years' presidential addresses to the Congress, I set out to explore the American Promise. I pre-processed and trained the speeches on a Gensim model in Python to return most similar words to each of these five promises: American, Equality, Life, Liberty and Happiness.
